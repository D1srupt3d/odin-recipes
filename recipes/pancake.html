Pancake

Description

Pancake is a flat, round cake that is made with flour, eggs, milk, and butter. It is typically cooked on a hot griddle or pan.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, eggs, milk, and butter
  2. Cook the pancake on a hot griddle or pan
  3. Serve the pancake
