Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that is made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and sauce. It is typically baked in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Cook the meat
  3. Cook the cheese
  4. Cook the sauce
  5. Bake the lasagna
  6. Serve the lasagna
